Cooking Etiquette

When you are the kitchen, there are some do's and don'ts. While some easily recognizable, others have to be talked about or even written. Cookin etiquette simply are the rules that guide the kitchen. A whole lot of events are carried out in the kitchen like butchering, slicling, chopping, cooking, frying, etc and with all these ativities follows the instruments used to carry out them.


Cooking Do's

  1. Always wash your hands, surfaces and equipments before cooking.
  2. Always walk with your pointed knives facing down.
  3. Treat your knives with respect.
  4. Wash up to avoid cross contamination.

Cooking Don'ts

  1. Never forgt about food presentation and portions.
  2. Don't dump rotten food in the thrash.
  3. Always clean up after cooking.
