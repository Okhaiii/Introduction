FOX's COOKIES

Every story has to start somewhere, and ours began with brandy snaps. In 1853, Michael Spedding baked his very batch and within a few years, he was selling to -shows,shops and traders from his Batley bakery. After Michael's son-in-law, Fred Ellis Fox, took over in 1897, he renamed it F E Fox & Co - and that's where we get our name.
